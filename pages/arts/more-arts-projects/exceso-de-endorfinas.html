EXCESO DE ENDORFINAS

(2016) (Direction) (Screenplay)

Exceso de Endorfinas is a short film that explores the feelings that I lived as a teenager in my first relationship. Represents the loss and sadness in a cruel vision.

Won Best Screenplay and Best Production Design at the high school student project festival.

This experience made me interested in cinema and writting.
